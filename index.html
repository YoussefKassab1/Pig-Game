How to Play

🎲 Roll the dice as many times as you like to add to your Current score.

⚡ If you roll a 1, you lose your Current score and it's the next player's turn.

📥 Click Hold to add your Current score to your Total score.

🏆 First player to reach 100 points wins!

NOTE!!! (This game can't be played online but on the same device Only)
